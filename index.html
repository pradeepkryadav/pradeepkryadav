About Me

With hands-on experience in technologies like Java, .Net, C++, Python, Mahcine Learning, Artificial intelligence, database like Oracle, HANA, Mysql and various cloud platforms, I specialize in solving complex performance challenges, designing cost-effective solutions, and driving impactful results for large-scale systems. I take pride in my ability to combine technical expertise with strategic thinking to deliver high-performance, scalable, and reliable solutions.
